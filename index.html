prop·a·gan·da

information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote or publicize a particular political cause or point of view

What is Prop Stop?

Prop Stop (Propaganda Stopper) is a tool prevent the spread and consumption of disinformation on the Internet.

Why?

Because we're all sick of our Uncle's polarizing, propagandist Facebook posts and it's time to do something about it.

How does it work?

It's simple.

When you see propaganda, you report it and our community of fact-checkers rate it.

That propaganda - be it text or image - will now carry a watermark with it, anywhere that it appears on the Internet, alerting the viewer to its insidious nature.
